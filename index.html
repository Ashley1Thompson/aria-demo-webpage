Welcome to the ARIA Demonstration Homepage!

On this site you will find two versions of content that appear visually identical. However, to a user of assitive technology, the two versions could not be more different. One uses ARIA (Accessible Rich Internet Applications) properties appropriately, making the content accessible, while the other does not.

To use this demonstration page, please enable a screen reader in your browser.

Using ARIA for Accordions

Proper use of ARIA with accordions should mean that a screen reader will announce the accordion header button that controls visibility of the content within, whether or not that region of the accordion is expanded or collapsed, and allows the user to navigate into and out of the accordion and its content using a keyboard.
